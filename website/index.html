Snappy Decisions

About

What should I have for dinner? Should I go out tonight? Should I get married? Life is filled with difficult decisions that only cause stress and worry. With Snappy Decisions you can avoid taking any personal responsibility and let your phone choose for you.

Snappy Decisions can help with any type of decision. Use the random decider to pick from a set of user defined options. Use the food decider to pick which restaraunt to go to. The product decider will help you make an informed decision by looking up relevant product info.

Random Decision

The end-all decision maker. Add potential choices and we'll randomly pick one for you. Need more control? Add weighting to favor one choice more than the others. Is there a set of choices you want to decide from frequently? Save it now and easily reload it again later.

Food

Can't decide where to eat? Our automatic search will find nearby restaraunts and randomly pick one for you. Don't like our pick? Adjust the search radius or add filters to restrict food type and pricing. Unsure about the restaurant? Open up the Yelp page to get more information. Want to go there? Easily open directions in maps and be on your way!

Products

Need helping picking out a new phone? Add the different products you are considering and easily load relevant info from popular shopping websites like Amazon, Newegg, and eBay.
